Biography

Nani is an Indian actor, film producer and television presenter who works predominantly in Telugu films — considered one of the highest-paid and leading actors of Telugu Cinema. He made his debut with Mohana Krishna Indraganti's 2008 comedy Ashta Chamma, an adaptation of Oscar Wilde's The Importance of Being Earnest. After its commercial success, Nani collaborated with luminaries like S. S. Rajamouli and Gautham Vasudev Menon, earning the Nandi Award for Best Actor for Yeto Vellipoyindhi Manasu and an award at the 2013 Toronto After Dark Film Festival for Eega. His blockbuster run with Bhale Bhale Magadivoy, Jersey, and beyond cemented his place as a generational force in Indian cinema.
